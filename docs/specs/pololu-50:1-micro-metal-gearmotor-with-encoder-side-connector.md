# Pololu 50:1 Micro Metal Gearmotor HP 6V with 12 CPR Encoder, Side Connector

> **Source:** [Pololu Product Page](https://www.pololu.com/product/4844)
> **Category:** Micro Metal Gearmotors

---

## Overview

This is a miniature brushed DC metal gearmotor with a gearbox cross section of 10×12 mm and a 9 mm long, 3 mm diameter D-shaped gearbox output shaft. It features an integrated two-channel incremental Hall effect encoder that senses the rotation of a magnetic disk on the rear protrusion of the motor shaft.

The encoder is connected via a **6-pin JST SH-style male connector** oriented perpendicularly to the motor (side connector), providing versatile mounting options.

## Key Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Motor/brush type** | HP 6V: high-power 6V with precious metal brushes |
| **Gear ratio** | 51.45:1 (nominally 50:1) |
| **Operating voltage** | 6V |
| **No-load speed** | ~590 RPM |
| **No-load current** | ~100 mA |
| **Stall torque** | ~0.86 kg·cm (11 oz·in) |
| **Stall current** | ~1.6 A |
| **Encoder resolution** | 12 CPR (motor shaft) / ~617 CPR (gearbox output shaft) |

> **Note:** Stall torque and stall current specifications are theoretical values; stalls could damage the motor or gearbox.

## Gearbox Details

- **Cross section:** 10 × 12 mm
- **Output shaft:** 9 mm long, 3 mm diameter, D-shaped
- **Gearbox type:** Metal gears with high-durability steel plates

## Encoder Details

- **Type:** Two-channel incremental Hall effect encoder
- **Resolution:** 12 counts per revolution (CPR) of the motor shaft
- **Gearbox output CPR:** ~617 CPR (12 CPR × 51.45)
- **Connector:** 6-pin JST SH-style male connector (side-entry)
- **Operating voltage:** 2.7V – 18V

### Encoder Pinout (6-pin JST SH connector)

| Pin | Function |
|-----|----------|
| 1 | Motor power (connects to one motor terminal) |
| 2 | Encoder output B |
| 3 | Encoder Vcc (2.7V – 18V) |
| 4 | Encoder GND |
| 5 | Encoder output A |
| 6 | Motor power (connects to other motor terminal) |

## Dimensions

- **Gearbox cross section:** 10 × 12 mm
- **Output shaft length:** 9 mm
- **Output shaft diameter:** 3 mm (D-shaped)

## Accessories

Compatible accessories include:

## Notes

- The side connector orientation offers versatile mounting options compared to back-entry versions
